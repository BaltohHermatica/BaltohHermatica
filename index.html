Mission

Baltoh Hermatica is a mystery school devoted to guiding individuals into harmony with the Hermatic principles that shape existence. Through the practice of justice, the protection of liberty, and a deep commitment to self-knowledge, it cultivates inner transformation and a conscious alignment with the universal order.

About Us

Baltoh Hermatica is a not-for-profit organisation firmly grounded in the Western Esoteric Tradition, drawing from a lineage of thought that has long sought to understand the hidden structures behind existence. It exists as a disciplined rosicrucian order, shaped by the enduring principles of Hermeticism, enriched by the transformative path of Alchemy, and illuminated through the symbolic and contemplative depth of Qabbalah, Tarot and Astrology.

Within this framework, Baltoh Hermatica is devoted to more than abstract study- it is committed to structured growth, where knowledge is approached with intention, order, and continuity. It fosters a path of inner development that calls for both intellectual engagement and personal refinement, guiding individuals toward a deeper understanding of themselves and the forces that govern reality. At its core, the organisation seeks to cultivate balance within the individual, encouraging a state of harmony between thought, will, and action as part of a broader alignment with universal principles.

Meetings

  • Online meetings are held on the second saturday of each month and are open to non-members, offering an opportunity for those outside the order to engage with its teachings and perspectives.
  • Study sessions, referred to Pronaos, are reserved exclusively for registered members. These sessions form a more focused and structured environment, intended for deeper study, guided practice, and committed inner work within the order.

Resources

Each student is provided with a steady stream of structured teachings, writings and studies, delivered weekly. These materials are carefully arranged to support gradual learning, allowing the student to engage with the principles in a disciplined and progressive manner while deepening both understanding and practice overtime.
